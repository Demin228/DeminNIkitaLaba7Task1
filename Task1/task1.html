Air Max

This is the best and most numerous line of Nike sneakers, the history of which dates back to 1979. It became a true revolutionary breakthrough: an unusual foam in the sole, which sagged or deformed over time, the inventors introduced a special cushion with air.

It wasn't until 1987 that the Air Max was completely redesigned and redesigned, though. To make the air cushion visible, a special hole was made in the sole in the heel area, and it became a real sensation.

Cortez

The Nike Cortez line of running shoes appeared even earlier - back in 1972. It was then that the American Olympians first performed in Nike sneakers at the Olympic Games in Munich.aterial of different densities.

The old Nike sneakers were made of leather, now replaced by lighter nylon, which dries almost instantly, or suede, which perfectly holds the shape of the cape.

Blazer

Nike Blazer is a real legend in the world of basketball and a harbinger of sneakers. This line of shoes was launched almost immediately after Cortez - in 1973. Now it's a classic: this style of sneakers with a large emblem on a white flat sole can be seen on athletes from all over the world.

This model gained wide popularity after basketball competitions, in which sports star George "Iceman" Gervin performed in Nike Blazer. A slightly high bootleg was something original then, so basketball fans appreciated this move.

Air Jordans

The Air Jordans lineup is another iconic collection that first appeared in 1984 and was originally designed exclusively for basketball players.In those years, most basketball shoes were white, but the bold combination of black and red changed this trend once and for all.

Designed with mid-cut shafts, a flat tongue and leather inserts, this line has evolved over the course of the collection. The Infrared colorway is considered a classic, but now you can find other color schemes.

Air Force 1

The line began in 1982 and was the first shoe to use a cupsole sole.The white Nike Air Force 1 in the low Low version remains one of the best-selling ones of all time, but the high High version for the company became the first record for the price (over $ 1,000 per pair).

Although the designers took trekking boots as the basis, the sneakers were originally intended for playing basketball. Now they can be found in the catalogs of fashionable street brands. They are very much in demand in the pop and hip-hop industry and have long been an integral part of the sneaker culture.

Nike Dunk

The Nike Dunk was born in 1985, and although it was originally designed for playing on the basketball court, it later became the progenitor of the future models of the Nike Skateboarding series.The insole has acquired an innovative Zoom Air cushioning system and the tongue has a soft lining.

The Nike Dunk Collection is the ultimate skateboarding shoe, but also suitable for extreme sports, including parkour. The specific tread pattern provides excellent grip on flat surfaces and asphalt, so they are widely used in everyday wear.

Nike Zoom

The first models of this Air Flight 96 series, released in 1996, interested basketball players. This was due to the fact that basketball star Anferni Hardaway won a gold medal at the Olympics while wearing these shoes.

The design combines classic Nike style from the '90s with modern Zoom Air cushioning. The multi-material upper is accentuated by rubber outsole wings that wrap around the foot on both sides.
